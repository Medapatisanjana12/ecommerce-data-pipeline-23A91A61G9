# Dashboard User Guide

## Accessing the Dashboard


### Power BI
File: dashboards/powerbi/ecommerce_analytics.pbix  
Requirement: Power BI Desktop (Free)

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Executive Summary
**Purpose:** High-level business overview

**Key Metrics**
- Total Revenue
- Total Transactions
- Average Order Value
- Profit Margin

**Visualizations**
1. Monthly Revenue Trend
2. Top Categories by Revenue
3. Payment Method Distribution
4. Geographic Revenue Map

**Usage**
- Use date filter to adjust period
- Click visuals to drill down

---

### Page 2: Product Analysis
**Key Insights**
- Top 10 products by revenue
- Profitability by category
- Units sold comparison

---

### Page 3: Customer Insights
**Key Insights**
- Customer spending segments
- Lifetime value analysis
- Repeat purchase behavior

---

### Page 4: Geographic & Time Trends
**Key Insights**
- Top states by revenue
- Weekday vs weekend sales
- Regional performance

---

## Filters Available
- Date Range
- Product Category
- Customer State
- Payment Method
